Facts About Poland

  1. Geography
    • Located in Central Europe
    • Borders 7 countries
    • Capital: Warsaw
  2. History
    • Regained independence in 1918
    • Invaded in by Germany in 1939, starting World War II
    • Joined the EU in 2004
  3. Culture
    • Famous for the foods Pierogi and Chopin
    • Strong Catholic traditions
